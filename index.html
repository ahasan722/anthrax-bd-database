Situation Overview (2025)

Anthrax remains a persistent threat in the flood-prone districts of Bangladesh. As of Q3 2025, surveillance shows a 12% increase in livestock vaccination coverage, yet cluster events continue to occur in Sirajganj and Pabna due to post-monsoon spore surfacing.

Case Definition (Human)

  • • Suspected: Acute illness with skin lesion or severe GI pain with history of animal contact.
  • • Confirmed: Isolation of B. anthracis via PCR or Culture.

Post-Mortem Warning

DO NOT OPEN THE CARCASS. Exposure to oxygen induces sporulation. Visible signs include bloating and dark, non-clotting blood from orifices.
